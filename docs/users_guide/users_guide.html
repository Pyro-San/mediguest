MediGuest

User's Guide

Introduction

MediGuest is a powerful system for tracking all aspects of a company's guest management. It is designed to make your life easier, by taking care of as much as possible automatically, and reducing risk of errors.

Overview

MediGuest is, essentially, a website. You use it just like any other website, except that a shortcut will be provided on your desktop, so that you don't have to find the website first. So, the general pattern of use is:

  1. Go to MediGuest by opening the icon on your desktop.
  2. Log in
  3. Do your work
  4. Log out
