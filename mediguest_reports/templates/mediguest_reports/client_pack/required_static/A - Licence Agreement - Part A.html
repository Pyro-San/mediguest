5. Ending the Licence Agreement

Grounds for ending the Licence Agreement

5.1 {{ org_name }} may end the Licence Agreement by issuing a written notice to the guest. This may be on one or more of the grounds listed below:

  • 5.1.1 the guest has failed to pay the charges which are due or owed in the manner outlined in this agreement, or owed to the hostel
  • 5.1.2 the guest has, in the opinion of the Hostel Manager, failed to comply with or breached any of the conditions of this agreement;
  • 5.1.3 the guest or his visitor has encouraged or caused damage to or failed to look after the premises or its fixtures, fittings or equipment or has stolen or removed any property from the premises belonging to the volunteer, staff, visitors or any other guests of {{ org_name }};
  • 5.1.4 the guest has caused nuisance to other guests, neighbours, volunteer, or staff, or has encouraged or carried out acts of harassment on the grounds of race, colour, religion, political beliefs, gender, sexuality, age, AIDS or HIV status or disability;
  • 5.1.5 suitable alternative accommodation has, in the opinion of the Hostel Manager, been offered;
  • 5.1.6 the guest, in the opinion of the Hostel Manager, no longer requires the services provided by the project, or refuses reasonable offers of re-housing, or fails to co-operate with the support programme or has ongoing needs outside of the range of support needs catered for in {{ org_name }};
  • 5.1.7 the guest has needs beyond the range of those provided by {{ org_name }}.
  • 5.1.8 if in the opinion of the hostel manager, the guest has not complied with or cooperated with resettlement plan.

Notice Period

5.2 {{ org_name }} agrees it will not normally give less than 28 days notice terminating the Licence Agreement except in exceptional circumstances as determined by the hostel manager. The period of notice will be set out in the written notice which will also give details on why it has been issued.

 

Exceptional Circumstances

5.3 In the case of disruptive, violent or otherwise unacceptable behaviour or actions, the notice given by {{ org_name }} may specify a shorter period than 28 days, provided the period specified is reasonable in the circumstances.

5.4 {{ org_name }} will also issue notice of a shorter period if proof of ability to pay charges (e.g. being in receipt of welfare benefit) is not given within a specified timescale or benefit entitlement has been withdrawn.

6.3 Failure to sign this Licence Agreement will lead to automatic exclusion and re-admission will not be permitted until the guest has entered into a Licence Agreement.
